What is TEDx?

TED is a nonprofit devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. It started out (in 1984) as a conference bringing together people from three worlds: Technology, Entertainment, Design. Since then its scope has become ever broader.

TEDx Varna

TEDx furthers the spirit of Ideas Worth Spreading by bringing people together to share a TED-like experience through a program of local, self-organized events. The conference will be held for the first time in Varna.

Location

The conference TEDxVarna will take place in the -
University of Economics – Varna, on 77 Kniaz Boris I Blvd. 9002 Varna
